Our story

From İzmir to Orlando — Two Generations of Tile & Bath

Buca Yapı opened its doors in 2008 in Buca, İzmir — on Türkiye's Aegean coast — and grew into one of the region's leading tile and building-material retailers, with a 43,000 sq ft flagship campus and showrooms serving customers nationwide as an authorized Seramiksan dealer.

Buca Tile & Bath brings that same family-run service and manufacturer-direct European selection to Central Florida. Every collection in our Orlando showroom is chosen for Florida homes: cool underfoot, humidity-proof, and effortless to maintain.
